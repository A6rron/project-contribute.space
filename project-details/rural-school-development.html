Rural School Development

Help build and equip a new school in a rural village to provide quality education to underprivileged children.

Project Details

This project aims to establish a fully functional school in a rural area where children lack access to quality education. The school will provide necessary resources, trained teachers, and a safe learning environment.

Goals:

  • Build classrooms and facilities
  • Provide educational materials
  • Hire qualified teachers
  • Organize extracurricular activities
