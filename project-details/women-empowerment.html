Women Empowerment

Raise funds to support vocational training programs for women to help them gain financial independence.

Project Details

This project aims to empower women by providing them with vocational training and resources to help them achieve financial independence. The initiative focuses on skill development and entrepreneurship.

Goals:

  • Provide vocational training programs
  • Support women entrepreneurs
  • Organize workshops on financial literacy
  • Promote gender equality and women's rights
