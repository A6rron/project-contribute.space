Green India Campaign

Help plant trees and create green spaces in urban areas to combat pollution and promote a healthier environment.

Project Details

This campaign aims to enhance urban greenery by planting trees and creating green spaces. The initiative will involve community participation to ensure sustainable growth and maintenance of these green areas.

Goals:

  • Plant trees in urban areas
  • Create community gardens
  • Organize awareness programs on environmental conservation
  • Promote biodiversity and improve air quality
